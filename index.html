About Brankley Farm

Grown almost entirely through recommendation

Brankley Farm Kennels & Cattery first started life at Kelmash Hall in 2005, before moving to its current home at Brankley Farm. The kennels are especially large, giving every dog room to feel at home, and prices include heating, bedding, administration of medication and our own food (should your pet need a special diet, just bring their own). We're proud to have grown mainly through recommendation, and to have been accredited as a 5-star boarding establishment.
